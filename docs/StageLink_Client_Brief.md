## 1. Executive Summary

**StageLink** is a digital platform connecting audiences with local and university theater productions in Metro Manila. It serves as a unified discovery layer, bridging the gap between theater groups (producers) and theater enthusiasts (audience).

Unlike traditional ticketing platforms that focus solely on transactions, StageLink emphasizes **community, discovery, and engagement**. It aims to be the "MyAnimeList for Theater," where users not only buy tickets but also build a reputation, share reviews, and connect with other fans.

## 2. The Vision: "MyAnimeList for Theater"

The core vision is to transform the theater-going experience from a solitary transaction into a social, gamified journey.

### Key Pillars:
*   **The Social Portal:** A "Lobby" experience that welcomes users into a community. Dark mode, elegant design, and live activity feeds create a buzzing atmosphere.
*   **Gamification ("The Prestige Path"):** Users earn XP and level up from "Newcomer" to "Patron of the Arts" by engaging with the platform (buying tickets, writing reviews, commenting).
*   **Unified Discovery:** A single destination to find shows across Metro Manila, regardless of the production scale (professional or university).

## 3. The Manila Context & Competition

**Market:** Metro Manila (e.g., Makati, Quezon City, Manila).
**Competition:** TicketWorld, TicketNet.

### Competitive Advantage:
While TicketWorld and TicketNet are purely transactional platforms for purchasing tickets, **StageLink** differentiates itself by:
*   **Community First:** Focusing on the *social* aspect of theaterâ€”reviews, ratings, and user profiles.
*   **Discovery Layer:** Aggregating shows from smaller, independent, and university groups that may not be listed on major ticketing sites.
*   **Producer Empowerment:** Providing tools for theater groups to manage their brand, cast lists, and audience engagement directly.

StageLink acts as a **marketing and community engine** that can link out to official ticketing pages (including TicketWorld/TicketNet) or facilitate direct sales for smaller productions.
